Cookie Policy.

Last updated: May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Holkam Advisory (Pty) Ltd uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit holkamadvisory.com. We believe in transparency, so this policy is intentionally written in plain language.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember certain information about your visit — such as your preferences or actions — across pages or sessions.

Cookies We Use

This website uses minimal cookies. We currently use:

Analytics cookies — We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and interact with our website. This helps us improve the user experience. Google Analytics data is anonymised and aggregated; it does not personally identify you.

Essential cookies — Our website may use session cookies that are strictly necessary for the site to function correctly. These expire when you close your browser and do not track you across other sites.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party remarketing technologies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some website features.

For Google Analytics specifically, you can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
