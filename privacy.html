Privacy Policy.

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Holkam Advisory (Pty) Ltd (“Holkam Advisory”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit holkamadvisory.com or engage with our services.

Information We Collect

This website collects minimal personal data. When you contact us via our enquiry form or email, we collect your name and email address for the sole purpose of responding to your consultation request. We do not collect payment information, and we do not require account creation to access our website.

We may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website. This data is aggregated and anonymised — it does not identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide when contacting us is used exclusively to follow up on your enquiry and arrange a consultation. We will never use your contact details for unsolicited marketing, and we will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We apply industry-standard security practices to protect any information you share with us. All electronic communications are transmitted over encrypted connections. Client relationships and intelligence are held in strict confidence as a matter of professional principle, not merely legal obligation.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use Google Fonts (loaded from Google servers) and Google Analytics. These services are governed by their own privacy policies. We do not sell your data to any third-party advertising networks.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@holkamadvisory.com.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at info@holkamadvisory.com.
